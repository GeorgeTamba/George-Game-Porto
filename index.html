About

I am a game developer enthusiast who enjoys creating games across different platforms — from 2D and 3D to Virtual Reality. I also explore 3D modeling and animation as part of my journey toward becoming a versatile game developer.

Based in Bekasi, Indonesia, I primarily work with Unity and Blender. When I'm not making games, I enjoy playing sports and video games to unwind and stay inspired.
