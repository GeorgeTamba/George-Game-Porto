Raturu: Fever Home

3D Game Horror Puzzle

Project Overview

A psychological puzzle game that takes place inside the feverish dream of a young child. Players navigate a surreal, eerie environment, solving intricate puzzles while evading a mysterious presence lurking in the shadows.

Documentation

Key Features

  • Intense Horror Atmosphere
  • First-Person Puzzle Gameplay
  • Dynamic Level Design
  • Easy-to-Grasp Controls, Easy-to-Jump
  • Short, Impactful Experience

Technical Details

  • Role: Game Programmer & Level Designer
  • Team Size: 10 People
  • Time Frame: 1 Week
  • Engine / Tools: Unity
  • Game Platform: PC
  • Released: February, 2025

My Contributions / What I Have Learned

As the game programmer and level designer, I took on the making of the second level from the three level inside the game. Making a big room full with doors that can be open only by using a key and when the lights turned off. The players are given a flashlight in a dark room to find the key which is located in the center of the room.

The key learning in this project is that, I learned how to program game mechanics in a bigger scale of line of codes. The main thing I learned in this project is how I can work with a team of 10 people, most people think that the more people you have the less work and the less effort you put but in reality it is harder to keep up and to manage work with your fellow team.
